SMS Opt-In Policy

How TransVega RV and Truck Center collects consent and sends text messages to customers via the YardBoss platform.

Who Sends the Messages

Text messages are sent by TransVega RV and Truck Center using the toll-free number (877) 570-7836 via the YardBoss yard-management platform. Messages are operational in nature — no marketing or promotional content is sent.

How Customers Opt In

Consent is collected through the YardBoss online booking form (yardboss-deploy.vercel.app/portal/book.html), publicly accessible with no login required. On Step 2 ("Your Info") of the booking flow, customers must actively check the following checkbox before proceeding:

This checkbox is unchecked by default and must be explicitly selected. The booking cannot be completed without checking this box.

Opt-In Flow — Step by Step

1
Lot & Type

Customer selects a yard location, space type, and start date.

2
Your Info — SMS Consent Collected Here

Customer enters their name, email, and phone number, then checks the SMS consent checkbox shown above.

3
Vehicle

Customer provides vehicle details (make, model, plate).

4
Payment

Customer completes payment. Booking is confirmed and first text message is sent with gate code and space number.

Message Types

Customers who opt in may receive the following account-related messages:

  • Gate access codes upon move-in
  • Monthly payment reminders
  • Renewal due date notices
  • Move-out confirmations
  • Account status updates

Message frequency varies based on account activity. No promotional or marketing messages are sent.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP

Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe from all future texts.

Reply HELP

Reply HELP to receive support contact information.
