Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

TransVega RV and Truck Center ("TransVega," "we," "us," or "our") operates the YardBoss customer portal for booking and managing storage spaces at our yard locations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

When you book a space or manage your account, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, vehicle and trailer information, license plate and registration details, and insurance documentation. If you make a payment, our payment processor (Stripe) collects your billing information directly — we do not store full card numbers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your reservation and tenant account
  • Process payments and send billing notices
  • Provide gate access codes and security notices
  • Send account-related notifications by email and, if you opt in, by text message (SMS)
  • Respond to support requests

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you provide your mobile phone number and check the SMS consent box during booking or on your account profile, you agree to receive account-related text messages from TransVega RV and Truck Center, including gate codes, payment reminders, and account notices.

  • Message frequency varies based on your account activity (typically a few messages per month).
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages. Reply HELP for assistance or call (863) 600-3626.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Your phone number and consent are never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (such as payment processors and SMS/email delivery providers), or when required by law.

Data Retention & Security

We retain account information for as long as your account is active and as needed to comply with our legal and accounting obligations. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information.

Your Choices

You can update your contact information, vehicle details, and SMS preferences at any time from your account profile. To request that we delete your information, contact us using the details below.
